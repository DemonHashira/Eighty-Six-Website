Welcome to EIGHTY-SIX

86―Eighty-Six― (86―エイティシックス― Eiti Shikkusu?) is a Japanese science fiction light novel series written by Asato Asato and illustrated by Shirabii. It began publication by ASCII Media Works under their Dengeki Bunko imprint in February 2017. The series is licensed in North America by Yen Press.

Latest Volume

"Holy Blue Bullet" (ホーリィ・ブルー・ブレット Hōrii Burū Buretto?) is the twelfth volume of the 86 -Eighty Six- light novel series. This volume covers the eighth story arc of the series, depicting the aftermath of Operation Lorica Sacra. The Strike Package had been wounded mentally and required time to recover. However, a group of desperate fools drag them back into combat, all for a pie in the sky idea.
